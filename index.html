Project: Marina Mabrey Shooting Analysis (2024-2025)

This analytics project demonstrates my ability to acquire, process, and visualize complex data to generate actionable insights—a key skill for modern sports operations roles. Using shot-by-shot data from the official WNBA Stats API, I analyzed Marina Mabrey's shooting patterns and efficiency across two seasons.

Key Insights & Recommendations

  • Three-point specialist: Majority of shot attempts come from beyond the arc, aligning with modern analytics-driven basketball strategy
  • Corner efficiency: Shows elite shooting percentages from corner three locations—coaches should prioritize getting her these shots
  • Limited mid-range: Minimal shot attempts from mid-range areas, demonstrating efficient shot selection
  • Rim finishing: Strong efficiency close to the basket on limited attempts, suggesting value in creating more opportunities near the rim
  • Optimal usage: Best utilized as a spot-up shooter in corner positions or running off screens for catch-and-shoot opportunities

Methodology

Tools: Python, Pandas, Plotly, WNBA Stats API

Data Source: Official WNBA shot-by-shot location data for all regular season games

Visualization: Hexbin charts for efficiency analysis, scatter plots for shot distribution with interactive hover functionality
